# CabbageSEO — Scope Lock

**Read this every Monday morning. 30 seconds. Non-negotiable.**

## What CabbageSEO is

An AI marketing agent for Indian residential real estate developers. Inspired by Okara.ai but built vertically for real estate. Runs daily. Produces an Actions Feed every morning covering:

- SEO audit (site health, technical, on-page)
- AI visibility (GEO) across ChatGPT/Claude/Perplexity/Gemini
- Competitor intelligence (pricing, content, positioning)
- Content generation (blog/LinkedIn/Instagram/WhatsApp)
- Portal optimization (99acres, MagicBricks, Housing.com)

One chat interface to talk to the AI agent about marketing decisions.

## Customer

Indian residential real estate developers. Hyderabad, Bangalore, Chennai.
3-15 active projects. Marketing budget 20L-2cr/month.

## Pricing

- Starter: ~₹50k/month (1 project, basic agents)
- Growth: ~₹1L/month (3 projects, all agents)
- Enterprise: ~₹2-3L/month (unlimited projects, priority, custom)
